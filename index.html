SKRIPTS


Here is a list of some of my good skripts i've made through my minehut servers, these are just the ones that i still have atm,


Bank Skript:
A bank skript, where you can deposit and withdraw money,
every 1 hour, players get a percentage of their money in the bank, you can increase
that percentage by spending some credits (ingame economy), and so you get more money.


Pets Skript:
Pets shop, where you can buy pets to boost your gameplay,
there are pets such as money boost, which gives extra money when you sell,
ores, and other stuff, and you get a bank boost from it, you get about +5-10% interest in your bank,
you only get boosted by your pet if it's enabled, once they're enabled they fly next to your head, and you can
level them up, to get more boosts to improve your gameplay.


Coinflip Skript:
A coinflip command where opens gui to bet with other players,
it has very professional guis, it has stats about players such as,
winrate, how much they bet in total, how much money they got, other,
you can choose how much you can bet with players, from 10$ to 100M+, you should
at least bet 10$, you can click to remove how much you bet or add how much, ect
it's unique and well made.


Island Loader Skript:
This is an island loader system,
once you run the command /island create, it will open a gui,
to choose what type of island you want, then it will wait couple
seconds until it teleports you to your new island, there are 5 types of islands,
they're all well built and unique, once you create/delete an island it will log it into the
discord log channel, with all stats about you, location of island, player stats, gens, spawners, robots, ect.


Robot Skript:
This is a unique robot skript, it's a remake
of hypixel's minions, but it's made in skript,
it has a storage system, you place it and it will start,
mining all ores around it, and add it to the storage, where you can
collect them later, it has very professional animations, packets and other,
you can pick it up, in skript you can't save entities once the server stops,
so i had to make the robots update every 10 minutes to make sure they're not bugged,
the robot will have your head, this was one of my hardest skripts, specifically the animations part.


These were some of my skripts, thanks.
